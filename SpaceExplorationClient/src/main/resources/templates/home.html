Main Rules

01
Choose astronauts wisely

Pay attention to astronaut status - not all of them are ready for flight! Check their specialization and make sure it matches the requirements of your chosen mission.

02
Astronaut salaries

Astronauts receive their daily salary multiplied by the number of expedition days, regardless of mission success. Budget is deducted automatically!

03
Mission outcomes

Mission success depends on various conditions: crew skills, destination difficulty, and random events. Sometimes alien invasions can occur! Victory brings money, defeat takes it away.

04
Budget management

Keep track of your budget! If you don't have enough funds to pay the crew, you won't be able to launch the mission.
